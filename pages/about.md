      <h2>What The Site Is For</h2>
      <p>
        This site is designed to be more than a simple gallery. It is the public-facing home for collection highlights,
        Pokemon TCG updates, notable pickups, and the broader Double Hit Collectibles brand story.
      </p>
      <p>
        Every card shown here is intended to reflect a purpose, whether that is rarity, long-term appeal,
        collection quality, or market opportunity.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="about-showcase__panel">
      <h2>Long-Term Direction</h2>
      <p>
        Over time, the plan is to expand the site with collection-performance insights that compare purchase price
        against current market value, along with gains, losses, and return percentages.
      </p>
      <p>
        The larger vision is to show that the collection is being built intentionally and managed with discipline,
        not assembled at random.
      </p>
    </div>
